# The Forge

The Forge is a durable knowledge base for turning lived experience, operational judgment, systems thinking, memoir voice, and leadership philosophy into reusable artifacts.

It exists to produce three outcomes:

1. Clearer decisions.
2. Reusable models.
3. Durable artifacts.

Notion remains the writing studio: drafts, fragments, memoir pages, character notes, active excavation, and evolving story structure.

GitHub is the canon layer: constitutional documents, identity profiles, operating manuals, reusable models, and versioned snapshots that should outlive any single conversation.

## Core Thesis

The Forge exists to transform uncertainty into understanding.

It preserves complexity without worshiping chaos. It looks beneath events for systems, incentives, patterns, constraints, contradictions, and human meaning.

The working rule:

> Notion is where things are born. GitHub is where things become law.

## Promotion Model

Ideas do not become canon because they are clever.

They earn promotion.

```text
Conversation
    ↓
Observation
    ↓
Hypothesis
    ↓
Principle
    ↓
Canon
```

The Clerk may recommend promotion.

Andy approves promotion to Principle or Canon.

The full model lives in `governance/PROMOTION_MODEL.md`.

## Operating Principle

Every Forge artifact should answer at least one of these questions:

- What did we learn?
- What pattern did we uncover?
- What decision does this clarify?
- What system does this reveal?
- What should future reasoning preserve?

## The Short Version

Understand the human mess.
Find the system underneath it.
Turn the lesson into something durable.
